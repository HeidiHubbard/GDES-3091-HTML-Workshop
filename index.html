Background

Sir Winston Newton Lord Beaverbrook III, or Winston for short, is a Canadian Chocolate Point Siamese cat born April 17th, 2019 in Yarmouth, Nova Scotia, Canada. He is known for being the goodest and most fluffy boy to have ever lived.

Early Life & Family

Winston was one of a litter of six born in a sock drawer to a domestic cat named Milton. He has an older biological sister named Poppy, whom he lives with in Toronto, Ontario, Canada along with his adoptive parents Heidi and Hunter.

Genetics

Winston is a perpetrator of catfishing. When he was born, he was a luscious snow-white boy with black extremities. Since then, he has "metamorphosed" into a darker, brown coat. This change in coat color (and siamese's unique markings in general) are due to a mutation in the protein tyrosinase. Tyrosinase is responsible for making melanin, the pigment in fur. This mutation in the protein in Siamese cats causes it to work best at room temperature, roughly 25°C. However, temperatures in the womb are much warmer. Therefore, the melanin is blocked from making fur pigmentation during gestation. When Siamese kittens are born, they are stark white. However, when their bodies begin to acclimate to the temperatures of the outside world, they begin to turn brown as their body temperature cools, allowing tyrosinase to make melanin and therefore fur pigmentation. Winston is a literal shapeshifter.

Daily Life & Antics

Since the day he was born, Winston has been on a mission to live his best life. Though he is good at heart, he is driven by his curiosity and can't help but get into trouble. A notorious plant-killer, glass-spiller and bird-stalker, he is on the prowl day and night just causing ruckus wherever and whenever he can without a heck to give. Most recently, Winston was given a door-hang mouse to keep him entertained and out of trouble, which he proceeded to eat whole. This resulted in a 3 a.m. trip to the emergency vet and a hefty bill. Winston refuses to be tamed.

Hobbies

Winston is a jack of many trades. Some of his favourite hobbies include sleeping, grooming, purring, stalking birds, chewing on plastic, zooming back and forth between rooms, playing fetch, digging in plant pots, eating tuna, and scratching furniture.

Future & Aspirations

In the future, Winston hopes to become a successful hunter in the unforgiving Canadian wilderness. He has dedicated his life to studying wildlife, spending many hours daily watching 50 hour long loop youtube videos of birds on TV. One day, when he does not live on the 57th floor of an apartment complex, he will bolt into the great outdoors the first chance he gets and achieve his dream of galavanting among the birds, squirrels and mice, bringing back many feasts for his fellow family members. He hopes to be crowned the most successful bird hunter to have ever lived.
